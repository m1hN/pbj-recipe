Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Let's learn to make some peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Raspberry Jelly
  • 2 Slices of Bread

Instructions

  1. Lay out 2 slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one side
  3. Spread jam on the other slice
  4. Stick the two slices together and enjoy
